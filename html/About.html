VISION

"An office committed, sensitive and responsive to the social welfare needs of the municipality".

MISSION

"To develop, implement and monitor programs, projects and services for the poor, disadvantages and vulnerable sectors provided and efficiently deliver the basic social services within the municipality".

GOAL

"The poor and the disadvantages realize their potentials and becoming productive and contributing members of the community through the mobilization of different stakeholders in the convergence delivery of social services.

The Families and communities become empower through family approach and community organization.

The duty bearer formulated polities and measures addressing basic rights of children for survival, development, protection and participation.

The rights and privileges are duty promoted upheld and recognize for the vulnerable and disadvantage sector of the community".
